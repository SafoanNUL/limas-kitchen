A note from me

I'm Lima. I cook the food my family grew up on — and for years friends kept asking me to cook for their days too. So here we are.

Everything is made by hand in my own kitchen, in small amounts, on the day it's needed. Nothing sits in a freezer. Tell me about your occasion and how many people are coming, and I'll help you get the amounts right.

— with love, Lima
