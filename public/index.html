A note from me

I'm Lima. I cook the food my family grew up on.

For years I've cooked for our own dawats, Eids and gatherings, and friends kept asking if I'd cook for theirs. So here we are.

Everything is made by hand in my own kitchen, in small amounts, the day it's needed. Nothing sits in a freezer. Tell me about your day and how many people, and I'll help you get the amounts right.

With love, Lima
